# Product Brief

## Product Name

ClipMind MVP

## One-liner

An AI video editor that understands what is said and what appears on screen, then suggests cuts with clear reasons.

## Target User

Creators, editors, marketers, podcasters, educators, and agencies that turn long videos into cleaner videos or short clips.

## Main User Value

The user uploads a video and receives suggested edits such as:

- Remove filler.
- Remove repeated phrases.
- Keep important visual demonstrations.
- Find strong clip moments.
- Avoid cutting context.
- Explain every edit.

## MVP Scope

The first version should not compete with CapCut.

It should focus on:

- Uploading one video.
- Generating transcript.
- Suggesting cuts.
- Showing reasons.
- Previewing the result.
- Exporting final video.

## Out of Scope for MVP

- Multi-track timeline.
- Stickers.
- Effects.
- Complex transitions.
- Team collaboration.
- Native mobile apps.
- Full social publishing.
